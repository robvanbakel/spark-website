Features

Manage your employees

Keeping track of your staff is not an easy task. Spark helps you manage all your employees' personal information in one convenient place.

  • Change an employee's status to see who's available to work.
  • Leave optional notes about any employee.
  • Provide the amount of hours in somebody's contract. This will serve as a guide when creating their schedule.
  • Add new employees, which can then login and check their schedules.

Create and edit schedules

Create, edit and publish work schedules faster than ever while keeping your employees up to date instantly.

  • Use custom suggestions to quickly fill out details.
  • Leave optional notes for any shift. It's up to you whether or not they get shared with the employee.
  • Get notified when you're about to overwrite a shift.
  • Employees can synchronize their schedule with their calendar.
